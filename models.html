Synthesis Engines

STATUS: MULTI-MODEL OVERRIDE AUTHORIZED

The Pitch Engine does not force you into a single ecosystem. Your license grants you the architectural flexibility to route your extracted data through any of the three major intelligence providers. Use the 'Model Override' module to instantly swap your synthesis brain based on the complexity of your campaign.

Google Gemini [ DEFAULT PAYLOAD ]

The primary synthesis brain of the Pitch Engine. Optimized for extreme speed and high-fidelity contextual reasoning. Gemini excels at taking the raw, technical data scraped by the Extractor (missing SSLs, broken pixels) and instantly weaving it into aggressive, highly personalized cold emails that bypass spam filters.

  • Latency: Ultra-Low
  • Context Window: Massive
  • Best For: High-volume, technical cold outreach.

OpenAI (ChatGPT) [ ADVANCED REASONING ]

The secondary logic engine. Fully compatible with GPT-4, GPT-4o, and GPT-Turbo version strings. Switch to this node when running highly consultative, high-ticket campaigns that require deep, nuanced conversational tones and complex objection handling in the initial pitch.

  • Latency: Standard
  • Output Style: Highly Conversational
  • Best For: Enterprise-tier / C-Suite targeting.

Hugging Face OS [ UNRESTRICTED ]

The open-source bypass. For operators who refuse to be bound by corporate API guardrails. By plugging in your Hugging Face Inference token, you can route your pitches through uncensored, community-trained LLMs (like Llama 3 or Mistral). Absolute control over your own intelligence pipeline.

  • Guardrails: None
  • Flexibility: Maximum
  • Best For: Unconventional niches & extreme personalization.
